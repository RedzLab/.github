## Philosophy

> *We believe the best work happens when curiosity meets craft.*

**Open by default.** We share what we build. Knowledge compounds faster than capital.

**Move fast, stay sharp.** Velocity without sloppiness. Speed is a feature, not an excuse.

**Cross-pollinate everything.** Trading informs our AI. Security hardens our infra. ML powers our exploits. Every domain feeds the next. The walls between disciplines are where the interesting problems hide.

**Build in public.** The lab notebook is open. Our wins ship as releases. Our failures ship as lessons. Watch us work.

**Question the defaults.** If the answer is "that's how everyone does it," we're already looking for a better way. Convention is a starting point, not a ceiling.
